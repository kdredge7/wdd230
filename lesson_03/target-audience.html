Target-Audience

Persona 1: Jim Smith

Jim has been a long time resident in the Gem Valley area. Born and raised in the area and now raising his family in the Gem Valley. He has a strong passion and desire to work with and help others in the community. He is in his mid-forties and enjoys doing recreational activities in his free time.
He owns and manages an insurance company in the valley. He is an active member of the Chamber of Commerce.

Jim will use the site to:

  • Look at upcoming local events the Chamber of Commerce has planned
  • View the community calendar
  • Look at the directory to find other businesses to collaborate with
  • Share resources with other businesses to help them succeed
  • View information on becoming a sponsor

Persona 2: Kerry Briggs

Kerry is new to the area. She moved to the area, because her husband's job was relocated. She is a mom to three kids, ages thirteen to six, and runs a side photography business. She enjoys doing portrait and commercial photography.

Kerry will use the site to:

  • Learn more about the area and the community
  • Learn more about joining the local Chamber of Commerce
  • Join the Chamber of Commerce
  • Look for other businesses to collaborate with
  • Look for contact information of other businesses
